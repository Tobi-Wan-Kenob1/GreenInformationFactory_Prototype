4 · Combine policies and grants into scenarios

For your selected topics, the finder bundles the policies and grants that mention them. Tick the documents you want, name the scenario, and save it. Scenarios are kept in your browser (localStorage) — nothing is uploaded.

5 · Scenario metrics: funding, cost savings & CO2 mitigation potential

Potential funding is aggregated from the budgets of the grants in each scenario (shown as a 25–100 % range, since one consortium rarely captures a whole call budget). Potential cost savings (avoided ETS/carbon costs, fines, waste and energy costs) and the CO2 mitigation index are indicative, assumption-based ranges. Every scenario carries a confidence rating reflecting how complete its underlying data is — see the assumptions box below.

Developed within BioFairNet (Horizon Europe). Policy metadata © European Union, via EUR-Lex / the Publications Office SPARQL endpoint; funding data via the EU Funding & Tenders Portal search API. This tool is a research prototype: results are keyword matches, not legal or financial advice, and the CO2 metric is an indicative heuristic, not a measured value.
